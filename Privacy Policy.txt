Introduction

Biedrība “Latvijas Radošo savienību padome”  ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard information collected from users who visit our website, solely through the use of Google Analytics.

Information We Collect

We collect information about your interaction with our website through Google Analytics. This information may include:

Cookies: Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data about your website usage. These cookies track information such as the pages you visit, the duration of your visit, and your device's operating system and browser.

IP Address: Google Analytics may collect and store your IP address for the purpose of tracking website traffic.

Location Information: Google Analytics may also collect information about your approximate location based on your IP address.

Please note that we do not collect any personally identifiable information through Google Analytics, such as your name, email address, or phone number.

How We Use Information

We use the information collected through Google Analytics to better understand our website visitors and improve the user experience. This includes:

Analyzing website traffic patterns and user behavior.
Monitoring the performance and effectiveness of our website.
Identifying areas for improvement and optimization.
Disclosure of Information

We do not share, sell, or rent the information collected through Google Analytics with third parties. However, Google Analytics may share this information with third parties in accordance with their privacy policy.

Data Retention

Google Analytics retains data in its system as specified in their data retention policies. We do not control the data retention period of Google Analytics.

Your Choices

You can prevent Google Analytics from collecting and using your data by disabling cookies in your web browser settings. Please refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions on how to do this.

Security

We have implemented reasonable security measures to protect the data collected through Google Analytics from unauthorized access and disclosure.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our website and data collection practices. The updated Privacy Policy will be posted on this page with the last updated date.
